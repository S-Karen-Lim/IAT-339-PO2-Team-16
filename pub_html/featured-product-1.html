Cultura Coffee

Costa Rica: Medium Roast

$8.99

Cup Profile: Perhaps the most complex of our three roasts, this medium coffee blossom picks up on the bright notes of a lighter roast and marries them to the earliest elements of a darker roast. With balanced acidity and a smooth, full body, fruity and floral notes play around a bounty of caramel and sugar cane.
